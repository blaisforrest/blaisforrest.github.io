About the Photographer

More Than a Day Job

By day, Forrest animates the characters you see in your favorite video games. Off-hours, he turns that same eye for movement, light, and emotion toward photography.

Whether it's the golden light of a mountain sunset, the tears and laughter of a wedding day, or a family portrait that truly captures who you are — Forrest brings a storyteller's instinct to every frame.

Based in Johnson City, TN, available for travel sessions.
